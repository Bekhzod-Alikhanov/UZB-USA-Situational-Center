## 4. Russian-encoding guard

The May 10 commit `a379a07 fix(i18n): restore Cyrillic encoding in messages/ru.json` documents that any tool re-encoding `messages/ru.json` from UTF-8 to cp1252 produces mojibake. This audit's A4 fixes touch all three locale files (F6, F7).

Verification step after every save touching `messages/ru.json`:
